Design Choices

Color Scheme

Our color scheme is inspired by the natural beauty of the Eastern Highlands of the Manicaland Province area. We chose a deep blue for the header and footer, representing the mountains that tower the town of Mutare, and a yellow for the main content area, representing the vibrant atmosphere of the city. We also use a white and blue for text to ensure readability.

Typography

We chose two complementary fonts for our website. Our headings use a bold sans-serif font to convey a modern, professional feel, while our body text uses a classic serif font for readability and elegance.

Examples

Here are some examples of how our color scheme and typography are applied throughout the site:

  • The header and footer have a blue background with white text.
  • The main content area has a yellow background with white text.
  • We will use the following font family Roboto, sans-serif.
  • Headings use the sans-serif font in blue or white depending on background, while body text uses the serif font in white and blue text .
  • Links are underlined and use the yellow color.
